Course production worked beautifully at 200 courses and collapsed at 2,000. The catalog was the product nobody owned.

## Challenge

Metadata lived in slide decks. Every storefront re-described the same course differently.

## Outcome

One canonical catalog with 14k courses, feeding every storefront from the same source of truth.
