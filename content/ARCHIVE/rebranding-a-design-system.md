A rebrand landed while a thousand people were actively shipping against the old library. Freezing releases wasn't an option; drifting into two half-branded products wasn't either.

## Challenge

Six thousand assets referenced tokens that were about to change meaning. Every squad had its own fork of "the" component library.

## Solution

We versioned the token layer, shipped the new brand as a parallel theme, and migrated squads on their own release cadence — with automated codemods for the 80% and office hours for the rest.

## Outcome

Full migration without a single frozen release. One library again, and a token architecture that made the *next* rebrand a config change.
